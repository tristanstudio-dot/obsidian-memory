## Ежемесячные метрики

### Пользователи
- MAU (Monthly Active Users)
- DAU (Daily Active Users)
- DAU/MAU ratio

### Финансовые метрики
- MRR (Monthly Recurring Revenue)
- ARPU (Average Revenue Per User)
- Gross Profit Margin
- CAC (Customer Acquisition Cost)
- LTV (Lifetime Value)
- LTV/CAC ratio

### Качество
- NPS (Net Promoter Score) - цель: 30+
- Churn rate - цель: <5% в месяц
- Retention M2 - цель: 60%+
- Retention M3 - цель: 50%+

### Engagement
- Session frequency
- Session duration
- Feature adoption rate
